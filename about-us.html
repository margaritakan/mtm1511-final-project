About Us

The Rush Lunch is a small coffee shop in the downtown. The main audience is people who work in the nearest area and do not have a lot of time to sit and slowly have lunch. The company's credo: recharge people quickly and tasty.

The Rush Lunch's concept is a short time relaxing place with good food and friendly service in the hurried city. In this place, people can order and take-out or sit at the tables for a short time.
